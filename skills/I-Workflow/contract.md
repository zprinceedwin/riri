# Contract

Second stage. Lock the scope before any code is written.

## Purpose
Convert an accepted item into an explicit, testable agreement: what "done" means, what is out of scope, and how success will be verified.

## Inputs
- Triaged item with intent and effort tier

## Outputs
- Acceptance criteria (bulleted, testable)
- Non-goals (what we will not do)
- Verification plan (tests, manual checks, metrics)
- Owner (agent/role)

## Exit Criteria
Contract is written down and acknowledged. Mid-work scope expansion requires a contract amendment, not silent drift.
