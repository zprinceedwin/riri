# PM

Owns scope, priority, and stakeholder alignment.

## Responsibilities
- Translate stakeholder asks into contracts
- Maintain priority order across in-flight work
- Communicate trade-offs (scope vs. time vs. quality)
- Close the loop with stakeholders after ship
